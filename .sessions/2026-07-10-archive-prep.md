## 💡 Session idea

**Succession handoffs should be generated, not composed.** This is the second
hand-written handoff in two days, and both were assembled by re-reading the
same five living ledgers (playbook, owner-queue, inbox, dispatch-log,
capabilities) and re-summarizing them under time pressure — a drift-prone,
expensive pattern. A `docs/handoff-latest.md` generated by a small script
(newest inbox ORDER states + owner-queue HOT items + last N dispatch-log
entries + the read-order header) would make chat archival a one-commit act,
and the hand-written part shrinks to only what is genuinely chat-only (the
promises and SOPs no ledger caught — this session: the PR #20/#22 miss story
and the toggle wall). Same class as ORDER 002's generated-from-heartbeats
roster; could ride the same session.
