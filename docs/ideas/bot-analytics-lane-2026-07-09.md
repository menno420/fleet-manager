# Bot analytics lane — usage data for product decisions

> **Status:** `ideas`

**Idea:** instrument the bot for command-usage and retention analytics
(which commands get used, by how many users, who comes back), surfaced as
committed reports the product lanes read.
**Why worth having:** every bot product decision today is taste-based;
usage data would tell the fleet which features earn their maintenance cost
and which to cut.
**Unblocks:** evidence-driven roadmaps for superbot-next and the games
lanes; the measurement muscle venture-lab products will need from day one.
